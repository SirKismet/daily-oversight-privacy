Privacy Policy for Daily Oversight

Effective date: July 10, 2026

Daily Oversight is provided by Timothy O'Malley (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how Daily Oversight handles information when you use the app.

Summary

Daily Oversight does not collect, transmit, store, sell, share, or otherwise process personal information or usage data from its users.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal information, such as your name, email address, phone number, postal address, contacts, photos, location, financial information, health information, identifiers, or browsing history.

We also do not collect app-usage information, diagnostics, crash reports, analytics information, advertising identifiers, or device information.

Analytics, Advertising, Tracking, and Third Parties

Daily Oversight does not use analytics services, advertising networks, tracking technologies, or third-party software development kits that collect user data. We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, profiling, or tracking across apps or websites.

Data Stored on Your Device

Information you create or enter while using Daily Oversight, if any, is stored only on your device. We do not receive, access, or maintain that information on our servers.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because we do not collect or store user data, we do not retain user data and cannot delete data from our systems. You may remove information stored locally on your device by using any available in-app delete or reset features, or by deleting the app from your device.

Children’s Privacy

Daily Oversight does not knowingly collect personal information from children or from any other users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

If the data practices of Daily Oversight change, we will update this Privacy Policy and revise the effective date above. Any changes will be posted on this page before or when they take effect.
